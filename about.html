HELLO,
i'm Bryant

I’m an interaction designer not satisfied with screens, enjoy the journey of 0 to 1, and believe design can only make an impact when addressing both business and user’s needs.

I love systemic challenges and cross-culture design, so I helped Indeed to localize its enterprise platform to meet Japanese employers' needs. I joined IDEO to help China's business leaders unlock growth at scale. I pushed Mozilla's mission by bringing engaging web experiences to new platforms and markets, and I worked with HTC to create innovative solutions to put healthcare in the hands of individuals.

When I’m free, I love to get lost in weird places, finding the interesting links between bits and atoms and how they can do good to our everyday life.

I take freelance work this year. If you like to collaborate, just send me a note.
